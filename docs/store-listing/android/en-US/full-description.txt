Repolog turns field photos into submission-ready PDF reports in seconds.

Snap photos on-site, add date, location, weather, and comments — then export a clean A4 or Letter PDF you can hand in right away. No cloud account needed. Everything stays on your device.

KEY FEATURES
- Create photo reports with metadata (date, location, weather, tags, comments)
- Export polished A4/Letter PDFs with cover page, comment page, and photo pages
- Photos are never cropped — displayed in original aspect ratio (contain mode)
- Drag-and-drop photo reorder, camera or library import
- Home timeline acts as a logbook of all your reports
- Search, filter (All / Pinned / This week), and pin important reports
- Backup and restore via ZIP (manifest.json + photos)
- 19 languages supported
- Dark mode with System (OS auto), Light, and Dark options

FREE & PRO
- Free: up to 10 photos per report, 5 PDF exports per month, Standard layout, ads
- Pro: unlimited photos, unlimited exports, Large (1-per-page) layout, no ads

PRICING
- Monthly: $2.99
- Yearly: $19.99 (save about 5 months, best value)
- Lifetime: $49.99

WHO IS IT FOR?
- Construction and inspection workers who need photo evidence reports
- Facility and maintenance crews filing condition reports
- Freelancers creating acceptance or delivery reports
- Anyone who needs to turn phone photos into a professional PDF quickly

PRIVACY
- Local-first: no user accounts, no cloud sync, no data collection
- Location and camera access are optional — only used when you choose
- Ads (Free plan) use Google AdMob with UMP consent
