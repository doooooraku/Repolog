# IARC Content Rating Questionnaire Answers

## App: Repolog
## Expected Rating: IARC 3+ (Everyone)

---

## Violence
- Does the app contain violence? **No**
- Does the app contain realistic violence? **No**
- Is violence towards named/identifiable characters? **No**

## Sexuality
- Does the app contain sexual content? **No**
- Does the app contain nudity? **No**

## Language
- Does the app contain profanity or crude humor? **No**
- Does the app contain language that is sexually suggestive? **No**

## Controlled Substances
- Does the app reference or depict drugs, alcohol, or tobacco? **No**

## Gambling
- Does the app simulate gambling? **No**
- Does the app facilitate real gambling? **No**

## User Interaction
- Does the app allow users to communicate with each other? **No**
- Does the app allow users to share content with others? **No** (PDF export is to device/local sharing only)
- Does the app allow users to purchase digital goods? **Yes** (in-app subscription)

## Location
- Does the app share the user's location with other users? **No**
- Does the app access the user's location? **Yes** (optional, for adding to reports)

## Miscellaneous
- Does the app contain ads? **Yes** (banner ads in Free plan, with UMP consent)
- Does the app target children? **No** (under-13 excluded per privacy policy)

---

## Notes
- The app is a utility/productivity tool for creating PDF reports from photos
- No user-generated content visible to other users
- No social features
- No violent, sexual, or mature content
- Ad consent follows Google UMP framework
