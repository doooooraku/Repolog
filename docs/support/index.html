Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a report?

Tap the + button on the home screen, take photos or pick from your library, add a comment if needed, then tap the PDF button to export your report.

Do I need an account to use Repolog?

No. Repolog works without any account or sign-up. All data is stored locally on your device.

Where is my data stored?

All reports, photos, and settings are stored on your device only. Repolog does not upload data to the cloud.

Can I use Repolog offline?

Yes. You can create reports and export PDFs without an internet connection. Address lookup (reverse geocoding) requires a network connection.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

On iOS: Open device Settings > tap your Apple ID > Subscriptions > Repolog > Cancel.
On Android: Open Google Play > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > Repolog > Cancel.

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Backup > Export. A ZIP file containing all your reports and photos will be created. You can import it on the same or a new device.

How do I remove ads?

Upgrade to the Pro plan to remove banner ads and unlock additional features such as unlimited photos and the large PDF layout.

What languages are supported?

Repolog supports 19 languages: English, Japanese, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Korean, Hindi, Indonesian, Thai, Vietnamese, Turkish, Dutch, Polish, and Swedish.
