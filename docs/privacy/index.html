Repolog Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Repolog is a local-first mobile app for creating photo-based PDF reports. This policy explains what data is processed, where it is stored, and how users control it.

1. Data you create in the app

  • Report content (report title, comment, weather, timestamps)
  • Photos you take or select
  • Optional location data (latitude, longitude, address) when enabled
  • Backup files you explicitly export/import

This data is stored on your device. Repolog is designed for local use and does not require an account.

2. Optional permissions

  • Camera / Photo Library: used to add photos to reports.
  • Location: used only when you enable location in settings.

You can disable permissions at any time in device settings.

3. Third-party services

  • RevenueCat: used for in-app purchase status and restore purchases.
  • Google AdMob: banner ads for Free plan users only.

These providers may process data according to their own policies.

4. Data sharing

Repolog does not sell user data. Data is only shared when necessary to provide purchase or ad features, or when you explicitly export/share files.

5. Data retention and deletion

  • You can delete reports/photos in the app.
  • You can uninstall the app to remove local app data from your device.
  • Exported files remain where you saved them until you delete them.

6. Children

Repolog is not directed to children under 13. If you believe personal data was provided by a child, contact us to request review and deletion where applicable.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will update the "Last updated" date on this page.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at Repolog GitHub Issues.
