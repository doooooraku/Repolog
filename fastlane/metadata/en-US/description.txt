  Submission-ready PDF reports, built from the photos already on
  your phone.

  Tired of jobsite photos scattered across your camera roll, sent
  through chat apps, then re-typed into a Word document at night?
  Repolog turns the photos and notes you already take on-site into
  a clean, professional PDF — without an account, without the
  cloud, without the wait.

  Repolog is a photo report app for contractors, inspectors, and
  anyone who has to hand in a "report with photos" before the end
  of the day.

  KEY FEATURES
  ・ A caption for every photo — write a short note under each shot
  ("crack on the north wall," "before / after," "serial #1042") so
  every piece of evidence speaks for itself
  ・ Auto-filled metadata — date, location, and weather fill in by
  themselves so you can focus on the work, not the paperwork
  ・ Submission-ready A4 / Letter PDFs — cover page, comment page,
  and photo pages laid out the way reviewers expect
  ・ Evidence stays whole — photos are never cropped; original
  aspect ratio is preserved (no cut-off equipment, no missing
  damage)
  ・ Photo numbers built in — every shot is labeled (Photo 1, Photo
  2…) so you can point to "photo 12" on a call without scrolling
  ・ Home timeline — your home screen becomes a logbook so you can
  find any past report by date, name, or pin
  ・ Search and filters — All, Pinned, and This Week, plus name and
  comment search
  ・ Backup and restore — ZIP-based local backup keeps you in
  control
  ・ Works fully offline — no account, no sign-in, no cloud
  ・ 19 languages — designed from day one for international job
  sites
  ・ Light, Dark, and System theme

  WHY REPOLOG
  Other report tools push you toward cloud accounts, monthly fees,
  and approval workflows you do not need. Repolog stays focused on
  one thing: getting a clean PDF off your phone and into your
  client's inbox. It is the lightest path between "photo on the
  jobsite" and "report submitted."

  Most tools dump all your photos into a grid with no context.
  Repolog lets you write a caption under every single photo, so the
   PDF you hand in tells a story instead of leaving the reviewer
  guessing. And because photos keep their original aspect ratio,
  cracks, serial numbers, and before/after shots stay readable in
  print.

  Repolog is local-first by design. No user accounts. No cloud
  sync. No tracking that follows you across the web. Photos and
  reports stay on your device unless you choose to export them.
  Camera and location access are optional and only requested when
  you turn them on.
